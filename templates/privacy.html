Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

To provide our expense tracking service, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Your name and email address when you register
  • Expense Data: Amount, category, date, and description of expenses you enter
  • Usage Information: Basic analytics on how you interact with the app to improve your experience

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Store and display your expense records
  • Generate spending summaries and category breakdowns
  • Improve and maintain the service
  • Communicate important updates about the app

We never use your financial data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your expense data is stored securely in our database. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services for:

  • Hosting and infrastructure
  • Authentication
  • Analytics

These services are required to operate Spendly and are contractually obligated to protect your data.

5. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and maintain your session. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data at any time
  • Correct any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your data in a portable format

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please reach out through the app's support channels.
